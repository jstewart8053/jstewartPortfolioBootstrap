Full-Stack Web Student at Lambda School

Since June of 2020 I have studied full-time at Lambda Coding School and now I'm ready to turn full-time studying into full-time working. Since 2016 and up until Lambda I was a self-taught Front-End Developer who worked on personal projects in my free time, and worked on every Web tutorial I could get my hands on. I instantly connected with Javascript. The simplistic yet powerful language gave a dynamic life to my projects that kept me coming back. Discovering developer tools like SVG, Canvas, and Lodash made developing even more interesting. Learning to manipulate CSS to include rich user interfaces and gorgeous transitions really captivated my creative side. Since attending Lambda I have realized there is so much more to development, but those first skills I gained on my own have only excelled and prepared me in the Full-Stack course.
